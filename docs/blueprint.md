# **App Name**: CommerceLink Pro

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Detail Views: Browse a comprehensive catalog of phone products with rich imagery, detailed descriptions, prices, and specifications. Includes search functionality and filters by brand, price, or features to refine product listings.
- Shopping Cart Management: Add, update quantities, or remove items from the shopping cart. Users can review their selected products before proceeding to checkout.
- User Authentication & Profiles: Secure user registration and login functionalities leveraging Firebase for authentication. Users can manage their personal information and order history in a dedicated profile section.
- Dynamic Homepage: A responsive homepage featuring a carousel for prominent banner advertisements or highlighted products, along with dedicated sections for store location via an embedded map and comprehensive contact information.
- About Us Page: A dedicated section providing the business's mission, vision, and a brief introduction to build trust and brand identity.
- Simplified Checkout Flow: A user-friendly, multi-step process guiding customers through order confirmation and shipping details.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A confident and modern blue (#345EBB) to evoke trust and professionalism, aligning with a tech-focused, generic business identity. (HSL: 220, 55%, 45%)
- Background color: A very light, almost imperceptible bluish-gray (#F0F1F5) providing a clean, airy canvas that allows product imagery to stand out. (HSL: 220, 18%, 95%)
- Accent color: A vibrant and modern aqua (#3BB2E2) to draw attention to call-to-action buttons, key interactive elements, and highlight special offers. (HSL: 190, 70%, 55%)
- All text elements, including headlines and body content, will use the 'Inter' (sans-serif) font. Its clean, modern, and highly legible design ensures clarity and professionalism across the application, fitting a generic business and tech-oriented product display.
- Utilize a consistent set of minimalist, outline-style icons from a widely available library (e.g., Lucide or Material Symbols) to maintain a modern and uncluttered aesthetic throughout the site, enhancing navigation without overwhelming content.
- Implement a responsive, grid-based layout with generous spacing, focusing on clear hierarchy for product listings and content sections. Product images will be prominent, clean, and consistent in size and aspect ratio for a uniform visual experience.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects on interactive elements like buttons and product cards, and smooth transitions for carousel slides and modal windows, to provide a polished and engaging user experience without distractions.
